Welcome to LandCrusier Cafe

LandCrusier Cafe is a cozy and inviting spot to enjoy a delicious cup of coffee or tea. We offer a wide variety of options to suit every taste, as well as cow's milk, lactose-free milk, oat milk, soy milk, and almond milk. We also offer free barista-made coffee to guests who book a service with us. Our cafe is EFTPOS only, so make sure to bring your card with you when you visit.
