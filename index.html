Welcome to LandCrusier Cafe

LandCrusier Cafe is a cozy, vintage caravan serving a delicious cup of coffee or tea. We offer a wide variety of options to suit every taste, as well as small snacks and treats.

Our cafe is EFTPOS only, so make sure to bring your card with you when you visit.

We offer free barista-made coffee to guests who book a service with Mike Carney Toyota.
